Home: Flagged States
Categories are flagged to determine if they need to be ignored or fixed - or included again. The default state for a category is 'include'. Categories can flagged with the Category Summary page. Examine categories are flagged to be looked at. They may have been flagged by the application or by you as you looked the Category Summary Ignore categories will not be included in any summary of spending. Fix categories are to be looked at and changed in the source of the transactions (Mint, Quicken...) Include categories are to be included after having been flagged somehow. You can click on any category to get to its Category Summary.
